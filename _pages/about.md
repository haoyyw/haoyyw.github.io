Haoyu Wang, is a PhD student at the University of Sheffield.

His research interests include corporate finance, climate finance, and machine learning. His current work focuses on how firms communicate climate-related information, how textual signals shape financial and regulatory outcomes, and how machine learning can be used to study corporate behaviour and disclosure.

Current research themes include:

- Corporate finance
- Climate finance
- Textual analysis
- Machine learning
- Corporate disclosure

This website provides information on research, academic work, and related materials.
